So, this is finally happening, I kind of decided to start a weekly, maybe 2x weekly "show" consisting of my new and old favorite sounds.

Feel free to comment your thought, especially regarding the length which I couldn't decide what would be an ideal length for such a format. Initially I was thinking of 1h long sets, but then I went down to ~30 min. I think it's better to be a small set and packed with golden tunes rather than 1h or more and track quality going up and down - it is a bummer to listen whole sets for just a few tracks.
